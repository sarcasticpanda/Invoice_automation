**InvoiceFlow: AI-Powered Email & Invoice Automation**

---

**Welcome to InvoiceFlow**

InvoiceFlow is a specialized platform designed to automate customer support and invoice processing. Our AI agents handle everything from email categorization to complex product inquiries, ensuring your business stays efficient and responsive.

---

**The Complete AI Automation Suite**

InvoiceFlow offers a comprehensive suite of features to streamline your business communications:

- **AI-Powered Email Categorization**: Automatically sort incoming emails into product inquiries, complaints, and feedback.
- **RAG-Driven Responses**: Use Retrieval-Augmented Generation to provide accurate answers based on your business documents.
- **Automated Draft Creation**: Quickly generate professional, empathetic email drafts ready for review.
- **Quality Assurance**: Every AI-generated response is proofread by a secondary agent to ensure high standards.
- **Custom Knowledge Base**: Train your agents with your own data for specialized business knowledge.

---

**Frequently Asked Questions**

- *How does the automation work?*
  The system monitors your inbox, categorizes content, and uses RAG to pull relevant information before drafting a response.

- *Can I customize the agent's tone?*
  Yes, the system is designed to match different tones based on the email category (empathetic for complaints, professional for inquiries).

- *Does it support API integrations?*
  Yes, the system can be deployed as an API using FastAPI and LangServe.
