**InvoiceFlow — Company Policies, Billing & Support Guide**

---

**ABOUT INVOICEFLOW**

InvoiceFlow is an AI-powered customer support and invoice automation platform. We help businesses automate email responses, process invoices, and manage customer queries efficiently using Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) technology.

**BILLING POLICY**

- All plans are billed monthly on the subscription start date.
- Annual plans receive a 20% discount.
- Invoices are sent automatically via email on the billing date.
- Accepted payment methods: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal.
- Late payments (>7 days) result in service suspension.
- Accounts are terminated after 30 days of non-payment.

---

**REFUND POLICY**

- Full refund available within 14 days of initial purchase if unsatisfied.
- No refunds after 14 days unless there is a proven service outage.
- Partial refunds (pro-rated) are available if downgrading mid-cycle.
- Refund requests must be submitted via email to support@invoiceflow.ai
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.
- Refunds are returned to the original payment method only.

---

**CANCELLATION POLICY**

- You can cancel your subscription at any time from the billing dashboard.
- Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.
- No charges are made after cancellation.
- Your data is retained for 30 days after cancellation before deletion.
- You can reactivate your account within 30 days without data loss.

---

**INVOICE DISPUTE POLICY**

- Disputes must be raised within 30 days of the invoice date.
- Email disputes to billing@invoiceflow.ai with your invoice number.
- We will investigate and respond within 3 business days.
- Valid disputes result in a credit note or corrected invoice.
- Repeated billing errors qualify for a service credit.

**FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS**

Q: How do I get a copy of my invoice?
A: Log into your dashboard and go to Billing > Invoice History. You can download any invoice as a PDF.

Q: Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime?
A: Yes. Plan changes take effect immediately. Upgrades are charged pro-rated for the remaining billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

Q: What happens if I exceed my email limit?
A: You will receive an alert at 80% usage. If you exceed the limit, additional emails are charged at $0.01 per email, or you can upgrade your plan.

Q: How do I connect my Gmail account?
A: Go to Settings > Integrations > Gmail and click "Connect Gmail". Follow the OAuth authorization flow.

Q: My invoice shows an incorrect amount. What should I do?
A: Email billing@invoiceflow.ai with your invoice number and a description of the discrepancy. We will resolve it within 3 business days.

Q: Can I get a receipt for tax purposes?
A: Yes. All invoices include your company name, GST/VAT number (if provided), and are tax-compliant.
